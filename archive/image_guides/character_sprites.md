# Character Sprite Guidelines

Sprites convey facial expressions and emotional cues for spoken lines.

##Akane Hikami
- **Location:** `source/g_std/ch01`
- **Tag Match:** `[chara_show name="aoi" storage="aoi_001"]` → `aoi_001.png`
- **Purpose:** Frame emotional delivery in voiced dialogue.
- **Best Practices:**
  - If the sprite changes, expect a tone shift or emotional beat.
  - Use sprite data with audio to refine mood and emotional wording.
  - Don't assume tone without checking visual and vocal cues.

**Examples:**
- Angry sprite + neutral voice → sarcasm or internal restraint.
- Crying sprite + panicked voice → escalate emotional breakdown.
